## The Philosophy

Less friction. Every item on my desk should earn its place. If I haven't used something in a week, it goes into a drawer or out of the room entirely.

## The Gear

My setup is centered around a **47" LG UltraWide**, a custom 65% keyboard, and a single Logitech MX Master 3. The desk itself is a solid oak slab on IKEA legs — sturdy and beautiful.

## Cable Management

I ran all cables through a raceway underneath the desk. Only one USB-C cable sits on the surface, connecting to a 12-port hub hidden below.

## What I Removed

- A second monitor (added visual noise)
- A full-size keyboard (too much space)
- A phone dock (just set the phone face-down)
- A lamp (overhead lighting was enough)

## Result

My focus improved measurably. A clean desk really does correlate with a cleaner headspace — at least for me.
